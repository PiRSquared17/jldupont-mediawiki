=Feed Widget= This widget allows you to add any RSS feed to your wiki page. This widget uses code and service from [http://www.feed2js.org Feed2JS]. ==Usage Example== {{#widget:Feed |feedurl=http://feeds.feedburner.com/jldupont/mw-widgets |chan=y |num=5 |desc=50 |date=n |targ=n }} ===Parameters=== {|class=datatable ! Parameter !! Notes |- | feedurl || URL of the feed to show |- | chan || show channel info or not (y/title/n) |- | num || number of items to show (0 means all available) |- | desc || number of characters to show from description (0 means show full description) |- | date || show date (y/n) |- | targ || open links in new window (y/n) |} == Example == {{#widget:Feed |feedurl=http://feeds.feedburner.com/jldupont/mw-widgets |chan=y |num=10 |desc=300 |date=n |targ=n }} ==Code== You do not need to copy this; just provided as reference.
